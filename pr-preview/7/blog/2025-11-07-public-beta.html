Poaster launches Public Beta

Poaster Team
Changelog

We're thrilled to announce that Poaster is now in Public Beta. After months of refining our AI-powered tools, we're opening the doors to writers everywhere who want to grow their Substack effortlessly.

What is Poaster?

Poaster turns your long-form articles into viral social media posts using advanced AI agents.

You write the content, we handle the promotion. It's that simple.

How to Get Started

Getting started with Poaster takes just minutes. Here's a step-by-step guide with screenshots:

  1. Sign Up: Head over to my.poaster.app/signup and create your account.

  2. Connect Your Substack or Website: Upload your archive or connect your site. Our AI agents will analyze your content.

  4. Review Extracted Quotes: See the viral quotes our AI has pulled from your articles.

  5. Connect Social Accounts: Link your Hypefury account using your API key.

    Support for X, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TikTok accounts are coming soon.

  6. Set Your Schedule: Choose when and how often to post automatically.

  7. Add Poaster as a Subscriber: Poaster can automatically generate posts from new articles you publish to Substack. Add Poaster as a paid subscriber and any new articles will get added to your Poaster website, processed by AI, and if you have "Auto-Approve" turned on, they'll even get automatically posted to your social media feeds.

  8. Watch Your Growth: Monitor your subscriber growth and engagement.

Key Features

Poaster is packed with features designed to maximize your Substack's reach:

  • AI-Powered Quote Extraction: Our agents identify the best quotes from your articles that are likely to go viral, keeping your voice authentic.

  • Automated Posting: Schedule posts across multiple platforms without lifting a finger.

  • Multi-Platform Support: Post to X, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more.

  • Custom Branding: On higher plans, remove "Powered by Poaster" and add your own CTAs.

  • Analytics and Insights: Track performance and see what's working.

  • Works with Any Website: Not just Substack – extract from any blog or article site.

Pricing Plans

We believe growth should be affordable. That's why we offer plans starting at just $1/month:

  • Basic ($1/month): 1 website, 10 quotes, 1 social account. Perfect for starters.

  • Standard ($10/month): 3 websites, 1k quotes, 10 social accounts. For serious writers.

  • Agency ($299/month): 100 websites, 100k quotes, 500 social accounts. For agencies and power users.

All plans include automatic scheduling and premium support on higher tiers.
