Frequently asked questions

Your questions answered.
How does Poaster work?
Poaster connects to your Substack or website and uses AI agents to extract the best quotes and highlights from your articles. It then automatically schedules and posts those quotes to your connected social media accounts—keeping your feed active with your authentic voice, without any manual effort on your part.
Which social media platforms are supported?
Poaster currently supports posting to X (Twitter) via direct API integration and OAuth, LinkedIn via OAuth2, and additional platforms through Hypefury integration. Native direct connections to more platforms are on our roadmap. Check our public roadmap for the latest status.
Can I use Poaster with any website, or only Substack?
Poaster works with any publicly accessible website or blog—not just Substack. Upload your article archive or point Poaster at any website URL, and our AI will extract quotes and schedule posts from your content. Substack writers also get the benefit of automatic processing when new articles are published.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your paid plan at any time from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. We don't lock you in or charge cancellation fees.
What happens to posts that have already been published if I downgrade or cancel?
Posts that have already been published to your social accounts remain live—Poaster only controls future scheduling. If you downgrade, your account limits will apply going forward, but nothing already posted is affected.
