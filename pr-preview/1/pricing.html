Frequently asked questions

Your questions answered.
How does Poaster work?
Poaster connects to your Substack or website and uses AI agents to extract the best quotes and highlights from your articles. It then automatically schedules and posts those quotes to your connected social media accounts—keeping your feed active with your authentic voice, without any manual effort on your part.
Is there a free trial, or do I need a credit card to start?
Poaster has a free tier that never expires—no credit card required. You get 3 AI-generated posts per week and 1 connected social account so you can experience real growth before upgrading. When you're ready for more, paid plans start at just $9/month.
Which social media platforms are supported?
Poaster currently supports posting to X (Twitter), Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TikTok via Hypefury integration. Native direct connections to additional platforms are on our roadmap. Check our public roadmap for the latest status.
Can I use Poaster with any website, or only Substack?
Poaster works with any publicly accessible website or blog—not just Substack. Upload your article archive or point Poaster at your RSS feed, and our AI will extract quotes and schedule posts from all your content. Substack writers also get the benefit of automatic processing when new articles are published.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your paid plan at any time from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period, after which your account will revert to the free tier. We don't lock you in or charge cancellation fees.
What happens to posts that have already been published if I downgrade or cancel?
Posts that have already been published to your social accounts remain live—Poaster only controls future scheduling. If you downgrade, your account limits will apply going forward, but nothing already posted is affected.
